The whitelist and blacklist are case-insensitive.

{# Whitelist words #}
Only send tweets that contain one of these words/sentences. One word/sentence per line.
{# Whitelist words - regex #}
A more advanced way of whitelisting tweets. Use regular expressions to decide which tweets to send. One pattern per line.
Pro-tip: You can use ChatGPT or similar to generate these patterns. For example, in this case the pattern is 8[0-9](?:\.[0-9]*)?%|90(?:\.0*)?%.

The whitelist and blacklist are case-insensitive. The whitelist is checked first, then the blacklist.

Regex101 is a great tool for testing your regex patterns.
