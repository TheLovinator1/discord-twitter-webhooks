{# Link destination #}
Replace links to Twitter with links to Nitter. For example, https://twitter.com/steam/ will become {{ global_settings.nitter_instance }}/steam/.
{# Use Piped/Invidious for YouTube videos #}
Replace YouTube links with links to {{ global_settings.piped_instance }}. For example, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=whPh_PasI58 will become {{ global_settings.piped_instance }}/watch?v=whPh_PasI58.
